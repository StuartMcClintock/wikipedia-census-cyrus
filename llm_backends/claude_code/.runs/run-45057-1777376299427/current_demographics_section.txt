The [[2020 United States census]] reported that Cazadero had a population of 298. The population density was {{convert|41.9|PD/sqmi|PD/km2|sp=us|adj=off}}. The racial makeup of Cazadero was 258 (86.6%) [[White Americans|White]], 0 (0.0%) [[African Americans|African American]], 6 (2.0%) [[Native Americans in the United States|Native American]], 2 (0.7%) [[Asian Americans|Asian]], 0 (0.0%) [[Pacific Islander Americans|Pacific Islander]], 5 (1.7%) from [[Race (United States Census)|other races]], and 27 (9.1%) from two or more races. [[Hispanic and Latino Americans|Hispanic or Latino]] of any race were 19 persons (6.4%).<ref name=DP1>{{cite web |title=Cazadero CDP, California; DP1: Profile of General Population and Housing Characteristics - 2020 Census of Population and Housing |url=https://data.census.gov/table/DECENNIALDP2020.DP1?g=1600000US0612146 |website=US Census Bureau |access-date=June 10, 2025}}</ref>

The whole population lived in households. There were 146 households, out of which 17 (11.6%) had children under the age of 18 living in them, 62 (42.5%) were married-couple households, 17 (11.6%) were [[cohabitation|cohabiting]] couple households, 29 (19.9%) had a female householder with no partner present, and 38 (26.0%) had a male householder with no partner present. 49 households (33.6%) were one person, and 28 (19.2%) were one person aged 65 or older. The average household size was 2.04.<ref name=DP1/> There were 75 [[family (U.S. Census)|families]] (51.4% of all households).<ref>{{cite web |title=Cazadero CDP, California; P16: Household Type - 2020 Census of Population and Housing |url=https://data.census.gov/table/DECENNIALDHC2020.P16?g=1600000US0612146 |website=US Census Bureau |access-date=June 10, 2025}}</ref>

The age distribution was 45 people (15.1%) under the age of 18, 12 people (4.0%) aged 18 to 24, 65 people (21.8%) aged 25 to 44, 89 people (29.9%) aged 45 to 64, and 87 people (29.2%) who were 65{{nbsp}}years of age or older. The median age was 53.8{{nbsp}}years. For every 100 females, there were 78.4 males.<ref name=DP1/>

There were 245 housing units at an average density of {{convert|34.4|/mi2|/km2|disp=preunit|units&nbsp;|units|}}, of which 146 (59.6%) were occupied. Of these, 104 (71.2%) were owner-occupied, and 42 (28.8%) were occupied by renters.<ref name=DP1/>

The Cazadero zip code, 95421, has a population of about 1,592, and a housing density of 7 people per square mile.<ref name=USZC/>
