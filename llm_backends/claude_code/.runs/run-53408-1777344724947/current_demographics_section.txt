Calpella first appeared as a [[census designated place]] in the [[2010 U.S. census]].<ref name=2010CensusCA/>

===2020 census===
The [[2020 United States census]] reported that Calpella had a population of 799. The population density was {{convert|313.6|PD/sqmi|PD/km2|sp=us|adj=off}}. The racial makeup of Calpella was 442 (55.3%) [[White Americans|White]], 1 (0.1%) [[African Americans|African American]], 28 (3.5%) [[Native Americans in the United States|Native American]], 14 (1.8%) [[Asian Americans|Asian]], 2 (0.3%) [[Pacific Islander Americans|Pacific Islander]], 206 (25.8%) from [[Race (United States Census)|other races]], and 106 (13.3%) from two or more races. [[Hispanic and Latino Americans|Hispanic or Latino]] of any race were 348 persons (43.6%).<ref name=DP1>{{cite web |title=Calpella CDP, California; DP1: Profile of General Population and Housing Characteristics - 2020 Census of Population and Housing |url=https://data.census.gov/table/DECENNIALDP2020.DP1?g=1600000US0609990 |website=US Census Bureau |access-date=June 9, 2025}}</ref>

The whole population lived in households. There were 279 households, out of which 101 (36.2%) had children under the age of 18 living in them, 138 (49.5%) were married-couple households, 25 (9.0%) were [[cohabitation|cohabiting]] couple households, 62 (22.2%) had a female householder with no partner present, and 54 (19.4%) had a male householder with no partner present. 64 households (22.9%) were one person, and 34 (12.2%) were one person aged 65 or older. The average household size was 2.86.<ref name=DP1/> There were 195 [[family (U.S. Census)|families]] (69.9% of all households).<ref>{{cite web |title=Calpella CDP, California; P16: Household Type - 2020 Census of Population and Housing |url=https://data.census.gov/table/DECENNIALDHC2020.P16?g=1600000US0609990 |website=US Census Bureau |access-date=June 9, 2025}}</ref>

The age distribution was 175 people (21.9%) under the age of 18, 79 people (9.9%) aged 18 to 24, 201 people (25.2%) aged 25 to 44, 216 people (27.0%) aged 45 to 64, and 128 people (16.0%) who were 65{{nbsp}}years of age or older. The median age was 39.5{{nbsp}}years. For every 100 females, there were 109.2 males.<ref name=DP1/>

There were 299 housing units at an average density of {{convert|117.3|/mi2|/km2|disp=preunit|units&nbsp;|units|}}, of which 279 (93.3%) were occupied. Of these, 151 (54.1%) were owner-occupied, and 128 (45.9%) were occupied by renters.<ref name=DP1/>
