Bieber first appeared as a [[census designated place]] in the [[2010 U.S. census]].<ref name=2010CensusCA/>

The [[2020 United States census]] reported that Bieber had a population of 266. The population density was {{convert|77.0|PD/sqmi|PD/km2|sp=us|adj=off}}. The racial makeup of Bieber was 78.9% [[White Americans|White]], 1.1% [[African Americans|African American]], 0.8% [[Native Americans in the United States|Native American]], 1.1% [[Asian Americans|Asian]], 0.0% [[Pacific Islander Americans|Pacific Islander]], 12.0% from [[Race (United States Census)|other races]], and 6.0% from two or more races. [[Hispanic and Latino Americans|Hispanic or Latino]] of any race were 19.9% of the population.

The whole population lived in households. There were 116 households, out of which 20.7% included children under the age of 18, 50.0% were married-couple households, 3.4% were [[cohabitation|cohabiting]] couple households, 18.1% had a female householder with no partner present, and 28.4% had a male householder with no partner present. 29.3% of households were one person, and 16.4% were one person aged 65 or older. The average household size was 2.29. There were 76 [[family (U.S. Census)|families]] (65.5% of all households).

The age distribution was 19.5% under the age of 18, 9.4% aged 18 to 24, 22.9% aged 25 to 44, 27.8% aged 45 to 64, and 20.3% who were 65{{nbsp}}years of age or older. The median age was 43.5{{nbsp}}years. For every 100 females, there were 137.5 males.

There were 146 housing units at an average density of {{convert|42.3|/mi2|/km2|disp=preunit|units&nbsp;|units|}}, of which 116 (79.5%) were occupied.
